# Project Mealodic

Mealodic (meal and melodic) is an application that helps individuals and families to create recipes, meal plans and even shopping lists. The goal is to create a plan of what you want to eat when, to make it easier for your life to be organized when it comes to food and to throw less food into the garbage.
